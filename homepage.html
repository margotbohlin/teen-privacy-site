What is Surveillance?

Surveillance on social media includes tracking your posts, likes, and even how long you look at something. This is often done through cookies and algorithms.

Example: TikTok tracks your scrolling to adjust your feed in real-time.

Why Teens Are Targeted

Teens over 13 are not protected under COPPA and are a goldmine for advertisers due to their high engagement.

Use extra caution with what you share! You're more vulnerable to targeting.

Real Consequences

  • College applications see your digital footprint.
  • Hackers can use shared info for doxxing.
  • Targeted ads can influence your mental health.

5 Ways to Protect Yourself

  1. Review privacy settings on every app.
  2. Don't share real-time location.
  3. Think before you post.
  4. Use encrypted messaging.
  5. Don’t click suspicious links.
